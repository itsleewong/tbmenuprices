Taco Bell Drinks Menu Overview

Taco Bell's drinks menu is designed to cater to a wide range of tastes. Here’s a breakdown of what you can expect:

1. Classic Sodas

Taco Bell offers a selection of popular soft drinks that are perfect for pairing with any meal:

  • Pepsi
  • Diet Pepsi
  • Cherry Pepsi
  • MTN DEW
  • Mug Root Beer
  • Starry

Each soda is available for approximately $2.49.

2. Unique Freezes

For those looking for something a bit more indulgent, Taco Bell's freeze options are a must-try:

  • MTN DEW Baja Blast Freeze: A tropical lime-flavored frozen drink that has become a fan favorite.
  • Strawberry Limonada Freeze: A refreshing blend of strawberry and lemonade.
  • Vanilla Creme Limonada Freeze: A creamy twist on the classic lemonade.

These freezes typically cost around $3.69.

3. New Agua Refrescas

Recently introduced, Agua Refrescas are vibrant beverages infused with real fruit pieces and green tea:

  • Strawberry Passionfruit
  • Peach Mango
  • Dragonfruit Berry

Available in a 16 oz cup for $3.99, these drinks offer a refreshing alternative to traditional sodas and are perfect for summer.

4. Iced Teas and Lemonades

Taco Bell also features a selection of iced teas and lemonades:

  • Brisk Mango Fiesta
  • Lipton Unsweetened Iced Tea
  • Dole Lemonade Strawberry Squeeze

These beverages are priced around $2.49.

Popular Drink Combos

Pairing your meal with the right drink can elevate your Taco Bell experience. Here are some popular combos:

  • Tacos + Strawberry Limonada Freeze: The fruity flavor adds a refreshing contrast to the spiciness of the tacos.
